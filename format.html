    Our Opening Fixture of the 2023/24 Season Announced

    The Young Prospect League (YPL) just made a major switch from the traditional league format to a super exciting hybrid cup format.

    It's gonna be off the charts! This new format combines the best of both worlds, giving teams the chance to compete in knockout-style matches while still having a shot at the overall championship.
